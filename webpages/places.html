  • Kalavantin Durg

    Kalavantin Durg or Kalavantin Pinnacle is located in Western Ghats of Maharashtra and was used by erstwhile rulers to have watch on surrounding areas. A series of rock-cut steps leading to the pinnacle were carved during the 15th century.

    With an altitude of 2250 ft, nearly 60-degree incline and steps being as high as 2 feet Kalavantin Durg trek is one of the most challenging treks in the Western Ghats. This makes it one of the most popular trekking destinations among youth and adventure seekers.
